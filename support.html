Support & contact

One inbox for everything: support@veridion-llc.com. A subject line from the list below gets you routed fastest.

CORRECTIONS

Spotted an error?

Accuracy is the whole product. If any fact, number, or attribution in an episode is wrong, email with subject "Correction", the episode, and a link to your source. Verified errors are corrected and acknowledged — that's a house rule, not a favor.

RIGHTS

Rights & takedown

All presenters are original AI-generated characters — no real person's voice or likeness is cloned. If you believe content affects your rights, email with subject "Rights" and we'll respond promptly.

PRESS

Press & partnerships

Interviews, media inquiries, or working together — subject "Press". Press bios and our transparency standards are published on this site and free to quote.

GENERAL

Everything else

Questions about the shows, the anchors, or how AI-generated news media can be trustworthy — we actually like that question. Subject "Hello" works fine.

What support can't do: give financial advice, discuss individual investments, or recommend anything with a ticker on it. The dogs don't, the desk doesn't, and the inbox doesn't either.
